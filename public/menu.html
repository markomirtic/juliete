Curried Chicken with Rice and Tomato Lettuce Salad

A hearty dish of spiced chicken served with fluffy rice, garnished with fresh lettuce and tomato.

Our signature curried chicken is slow-cooked with aromatic spices and served alongside steamed rice. It's paired with a fresh tomato and lettuce salad for a balanced meal that's both comforting and nutritious.

Decorative Citrus Fruit Platter

A refreshing mix of sliced oranges, lemons, blueberries, pomegranate seeds, and nuts.

Delicious egg noodles topped with crispy fried chicken, green herbs, and onions.
